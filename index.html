Data Protection & Research Ethics

Thank you for your interest in our 5-minute questionnaire!
Having a good night of sleep is very important for our mental and physical health.
This research aims to understand links between behaviours, physiology and sleep.
Should you have any concerns with poor sleep, please visit the official NHS page or SleepHubs for professional advice.
The International Sleep Charity is the data controller for your personal data collected through the survey and is subject to the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. All responses collected securely using Amazon Web Services. You have the option to leave your name and email if you wish to subscribe to newsletters from the International Sleep Charity and enter our competition to win a free mattress topper, but all personally identifiable information will be removed.
Please contact the lead researcher Yizhou Yu (email) for any information or concerns. Please note that all participants must be above 18 years old. The primary use of the data collected will be for scientific research, the results of which will be submitted to a reputable research journal.
